About Us

Entrepreneurship is the process of designing, launching, and running a new business venture. It involves taking on financial risks in the hope of making a profit. Entrepreneurs are innovative individuals who identify opportunities, gather resources, and organize them to create value in the market.

Starting a Business

The most obvious use is starting your own venture, whether it's a small business, tech startup, or social enterprise

Intrapreneurship

Applying entrepreneurial skills within an existing organization to innovate, create new products or services, or improve processes.

Problem-Solving

Entrepreneurs excel at identifying problems and finding creative solutions, which can be valuable in any industry or role.

Adaptability

Entrepreneurs must be adaptable and able to navigate uncertainty, a skill that's useful in today's rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

"We embrace creativity, collaboration, empowerment, inclusivity, integrity, continuous learning, resilience, community, impact, and excellence to drive entrepreneurial success, fostering a supportive ecosystem where all aspiring entrepreneurs have equal opportunities to succeed, upholding the highest standards of ethical conduct, promoting lifelong learning for adaptation and growth, encouraging perseverance through challenges, nurturing a diverse and inclusive community for shared learning and collaboration, committed to making a positive impact on society and the environment through sustainable entrepreneurship, and continuously striving for excellence in delivering high-quality support to our entrepreneurial community."
